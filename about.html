About Us

Who has extensive expertise in Nutrition Consultation & Weight Management.

Dr. Alpana Joshi, a professional nutritionist from Govind Ballabh Pant University of Agriculture & Technology, Pantnagar, Uttarakhand, a NET qualified professional Nutritionist in Food and Nutrition, Having a doctorate degree in Human Nutrition. How We Help

Everyone wants to have a fit and healthy body but staying healthy and fit is not so easy due to our fast lifestyle and improper food habits which have adverse effects on our health. We are very much aware of the fact that our daily choices determine how healthy we are, in spite of adapting to healthy choices we continuously ignore our health. The two important things which play a vital role in reaching our fitness goals are our diet and exercise. " We are what we eat" - this phrase is true as our physical and mental well-being is directly linked to what we eat and drink but by consuming junk food we create a nutritional gap which results in diseases,mental illness, weight gain and many more because nutrients serve as a fuel in our body and are essential for the proper functioning of a healthy body. *Healthy Biome* aims to provide a collection of healthy nutrient choices to people because our body requires a amount of nutrients which includes proteins, carbohydrates,water, fiber .....and all these are solely responsible for maintain a healthy and fit body. We put our clients at ease by helping them with their diet pattern, weight management.
